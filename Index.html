THE WEST AFRICAN EXAMINATIONS COUNCIL
Welcome to West African Examinations Council (WAEC) e-Registration Website.

WAEC Registers Candidates for Schools and Private Examinations.

The School Examination is WASSCE (for School Candidates) while the Private Candidates Examination is WASSCE (for Private Candidates).

Please Note that WAEC would not entertain any request for amendment of date of birth after Results had been released.
